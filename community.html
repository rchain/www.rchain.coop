The Power of the Cooperative

The purpose of RChain® is to provide its members discounted access to a decentralized, public computation platform. RChain supports the development of on-chain tools for governance and online communications that promote collaboration at scale. Our mission is to provide a platform that promotes the proliferation of social coordination technologies.

Join Our Community

RChain follows the International Co-operative Alliance principles. To learn more about what this means, read their Guidance Notes.

Becoming a member requires a nonrefundable, one-time $20 fee. To maintain your membership, you have to spend at least $10 in fees once the platform launches. You are then eligible for a rebate each year based on usage (think REI).

During registration you’ll have the option of joining our Discord community. Once you’re signed up, we’ll communicate with you through member newsletters to provide voting information and other essential notifications.

Engage with the Community

Community is the heart of RChain. The cooperative model unites us in building technology and social tools. Aimed at lifting our world up and effecting real, positive change, we will help each other flourish.
