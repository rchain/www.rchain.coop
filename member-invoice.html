Join the Cooperative

The purpose of RChain is to provide its members discounted access to a decentralized, public computation platform. RChain supports the development of on-chain tools for governance and online communications that promote collaboration at scale. Our mission is to provide a platform that promotes the proliferation of social coordination technologies.

Becoming a member requires a non-refundable one-time fee of $20 via REV payment or Stripe Checkout.

To maintain your membership, you must spend at least $10 worth of REV each year on contract deployment fees. You are then eligible for a yearly rebate that will offset these transaction fees. (The Co-op has not yet enforced this policy and has waived this requirement in past years.)

After payment, you will receive instructions on completing the following required steps:

  • Join the Co-op's guild on Discord where you can chat with Co-op members, developers, and staff.
  • affirmation of our membership agreement
  • Secure upload of the full photo page of your valid passport.
  • ID-Verification (over webcam) with Co-op staff

To pay with Credit Card, select Stripe

To pay with REV:

  • Connect with a Co-op Staff member on RChain's Discord Guild by posting @greeter in the #guest channel on Discord.
  • Be patient. Staff will reply within a few hours.
  • Communicate your REV address to the staff member prior to sending $20 worth of REV.
  • Staff will confirm the membership fee REV address ending in Qcn192Vm8.
  • The fee in REV is based off of Coinmarketcap pricing.
