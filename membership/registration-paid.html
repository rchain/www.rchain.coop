Check your email for further instructions.

To complete your membership, please join the Co-op's Discord Guild using this invitation link

When you first enter our Discord guild, you will be in the #guests channel and have only partial access to the Co-op's other chat channels.

Type @greeter in the chat box at the bottom of the Discord screen to alert a Co-op staff member that you are ready to proceed with your registration. Please be patient, it may be a few hours before you receive a reply. Be prepared for your meeting by having ready:

  • a download link to a secure copy of your Passport (see below)
  • a working webcam for live video-chat over Discord or https://whereby.com

Passport photo and secure link:

To perform a live ID-Verification on webcam, the Co-op staff member will need a link to a copy of your Passport photo ID page. During the live webcam interview, the staff member will use this link to access your ID-photo and compare it to your live face on webcam. We do not recommend emailing your ID or uploading it to Discord. The Co-op will not retain a copy of your ID after the ID-Verification is completed.

Mozilla offers a free and secure file sharing service that uses end-to-end encryption. We suggest you use https://send.firefox.com for sending your ID to the Co-op staff member who is helping you.

The file requirements for the image:

  • a copy of your valid identification in PNG, JPEG or PDF format, no larger than 3mb in size
  • The image must be high quality, unobstructed and uncropped
  • The image must show the full document page

Current valid passport:

  • Passport must be valid and display the full photo ID page with a clear facial picture of the applicant and the passport holder's signature
  • All passport details must match the details as submitted in the application, including:
    • First and last name of applicant
    • Date of Birth
    • Passport date of issue and expiry date
    • Passport number
    • The Machine Readable Zone (bottom barcode) must be legible and complete
    • The passport must not be expired
